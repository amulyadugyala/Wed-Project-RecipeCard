Chocolate Cake

Prep Time: 30 mins

Serves: 8

  • 2 cups flour
  • 1 cup sugar
  • 1 cup milk
  • ½ cup cocoa powder
  • 2 eggs
  1. Preheat the oven to 350°F (175°C).
  2. Mix all the dry ingredients.
  3. Add wet ingredients and mix well.
  4. Pour into a greased pan.
  5. Bake for 30 minutes.

Creamy Pasta

Prep Time: 25 mins

Serves: 4

  • 200g penne pasta
  • 1 cup cream
  • ½ cup grated cheese
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  1. Boil pasta until al dente.
  2. In a pan, sauté garlic in butter.
  3. Add cream and bring to simmer.
  4. Add cheese and stir until melted.
  5. Mix pasta into sauce and serve hot.

Fresh Salad

Prep Time: 15 mins

Serves: 2

  • 1 cucumber, sliced
  • 1 tomato, chopped
  • ½ onion, sliced
  • Fresh lettuce leaves
  • Lemon juice and olive oil
  1. Wash and chop all vegetables.
  2. Place lettuce on plate.
  3. Mix cucumber, tomato, onion.
  4. Drizzle lemon juice and olive oil.
  5. Toss and serve immediately.
